## Full description
Tactic Boss AI helps football gamers create smarter tactical plans for EA SPORTS FC, FIFA, eFootball and PES-style games.

Build game-specific tactical plans, analyze rivals, improve your own setups, use an interactive tactical board, explore community tactics and track your coaching progress with XP, levels and daily challenges.

Key features:
- AI tactical plan generator.
- Game-specific tactical DNA for EA FC, FIFA, eFootball and PES.
- eFootball manager simulation with manager playstyle, best formations and Link-up notes.
- Auto tactical board drawing with position abbreviations.
- Rival Mode for counter tactics.
- Screenshot Analyzer and AI Match Analyst beta workflows.
- Community Lite: publish, like, save, use and share tactics.
- XP, levels, streaks and Daily Challenges.
- Boss Coins economy foundation for future rewarded ads.

Important: Tactic Boss AI is an independent football tactics tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by EA, FIFA, KONAMI, eFootball or PES.
